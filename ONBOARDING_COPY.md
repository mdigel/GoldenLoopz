# GoldenLoopz Onboarding

## Intent
- Introduce GoldenLoopz as a discipline + accountability companion for side hustles.
- Ground the product in the “Golden Hours” concept (time carved out weekly to build).
- Educate with a short, friendly summary of the inspiration video.
- Capture initial goals for the three Golden Hours categories.
- Let users enable/adjust default habit metrics before they start tracking.

## Current Flow Order
1. Splash
2. Welcome
3. Video Inspiration
4. Video Sparknotes
5. Golden Hours Goals
6. Metrics Setup
